Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 08, 2025

Welcome to Faisal Sim Data. Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to protecting your personal information and details how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit and use our website, https://faisalsimdata.move.pk/ (the “Site”).

By accessing or using our Site, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not access or use our Site.

1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Faisal Sim Data, we are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy through:

  • Responsible Data Collection: We only collect information that is necessary to provide and improve our services.
  • Robust Security: We implement strong security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.
  • Transparent Practices: We are open and clear about how we use and share information.
  • User Control: We empower you with choices and control over your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better, more efficient service to our users. This information falls into two categories:

  • Information You Provide to Us: When you use our search tools, you voluntarily provide a mobile number or a CNIC number for the purpose of your query. If you contact us directly (e.g., via email or a contact form), we may collect your name, email address, and the content of your message.
  • Usage Data (Automatically Collected):When you visit our Site, we automatically collect certain technical information to help us understand how our service is used and to improve your experience. This may include:
    • Your IP (Internet Protocol) address
    • Browser type and version
    • The pages you visit on our Site
    • The time and date of your visit
    • The time spent on those pages

This data is typically aggregated and does not personally identify you.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain Our Service: To process your search queries and deliver the requested sim or CNIC information.
  • To Improve and Personalize Our Site: To analyze usage trends, understand user needs, and enhance the functionality and user experience of our Site.
  • To Ensure Security: To monitor for and prevent fraudulent or illegal activities and to protect the security of our Site and users.
  • To Respond to Inquiries: To answer your questions and provide customer support when you contact us.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We hold your privacy in high regard and operate on a strict principle of confidentiality.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing or any other purposes.

  • Service Providers: We may engage trusted third-party companies to perform certain functions on our behalf, such as website hosting or data analytics. These parties are given access to the information needed to perform their specific functions and are contractually obligated to maintain its confidentiality and security.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal process, such as a court order, subpoena, or a request from a government or law enforcement agency. We will only do so when we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to comply with the law, protect our rights or property, or ensure the safety of our users.

5. Data Security

We implement a range of appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, it is important to understand that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Your Choices and Rights

We believe in empowering you with control over your data. You have the right to:

  • Access Information: You can request information about the data we may hold about you.
  • Request Deletion: You can request the deletion of any personal information you have provided to us, subject to our legal and operational retention needs.
  • Opt-Out: You can choose not to provide certain information, although this may limit your ability to use some of our features.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Like most websites, our Site may use “cookies” and similar tracking technologies. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us improve your experience by:

  • Remembering your preferences.
  • Understanding how you use our Site.
  • Enhancing security.

You have the option to accept or refuse these cookies. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Site effectively.

8. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to other websites that are not operated by us. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party’s site. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the “Last Updated” date at the top. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or comments about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please do not hesitate to contact us.
